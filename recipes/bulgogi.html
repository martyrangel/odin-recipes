Bulgogi

Description

Bulgogi is a sweet beef dinner originating from South Korea.

Ingredients

The ingredients needed to make Bulgogi are:

  • 1 pound of marbled, very thin shaved steak
  • 1/4 grated pear
  • 1 tablespoon grated and peeled ginger
  • 1 grated clove of garlic
  • 1 tablespoon of brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • About 2 sliced green onions for topping.

Steps

  1. Combine grated pear, ginger, garlic, brown sugar, sesame oil, and soy sauce in a large bowl.
  2. Cut the shaved steak into narrow slices and add it to bowl. Thoroughly coat the shaved steak in the marinade.
  3. Cover bowl with plastic wrap and let it sit in fridge for thirty minutes.
  4. With one tablespoon of vegetable oil, coat a skillet. When the vegetable oil begins to shimmer, add half a pound of the meat.
  5. Move meat around with tongs until cooked, then place on plate.
  6. Repeat with the other half of remaining meat.
  7. Top with green onions and sesame seeds and serve with rice and Romaine lettuce leaves, or serve plain.
