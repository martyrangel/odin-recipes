Ham, Cheese, Apple Quesadillas

Description

Ham, cheese, apple quesadillas are a sweet and savory lunch food.

Ingredients

The ingredients needed to make some are:

  • 6-inch whole tortillas
  • Cooking spray or olive oil
  • 1 cup shredded low sodium Swiss cheese
  • 4 ounces thinly sliced Black Forest or Virginia ham
  • 2 apples cut into matchsticks

Steps

  1. Spray your tortillas with cooking spray or brush with olive oil. Lay them oiled/sprayed-side down and sprinkle half of the shredded cheese evenly on top of them. Layer with ham and apple matchsticks and then top with the remaining cheese.
  2. Heat a large nonstick skillet over medium heat. Lay a quesadilla oiled-side down and cook until golden-brown and the cheese begins to melt (estimated 2 minutes).
  3. Spray the top of the quesadilla with cooking spray, or brush with olive oil, and flip. Cook for another 2 minutes or until cheese is melted.
  4. Cut quesadillas into four wedges and serve on a plate. Enjoy!
