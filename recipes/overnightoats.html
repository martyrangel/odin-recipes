Overnight Oats

Description

Overnight oats are an easy-to-make, healthy breakfast food that come with variety.

Ingredients

The ingredients needed to make some are:

  • 1/2 cup of rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 1 tablespoon of plain Greek yogurt
  • 1 teaspoon of chia seeds
  • Honey drizzle
  • Your choice of topping, i.e. cut strawberries, fresh blueberries, banana slices, shredded coconut, chocolate chips.

Steps

  1. First, pour a 1/2 cup of rolled oats into a jar followed by a 1/2 cup of milk.
  2. Then, add a tablespoon of plain Greek yogurt. Do not stir.
  3. Afterwards, sprinkle a teaspoon of chia seeds on top.
  4. Then drizzle some honey to taste.
  5. Finally, add any sweet topping you would like, such as fresh fruit, chocolate chips, or shredded coconut.
  6. Screw the top of the jar back on and leave in the fridge overnight.
  7. Overnight oats can be kept for up to five days, so enjoy in one of the following mornings!
